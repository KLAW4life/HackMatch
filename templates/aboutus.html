Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to make hackathons more accessible, collaborative, and fun. By matching hackers based on their interests, skills, educational background, and work experience, we help you build balanced and dynamic teams that can tackle any challenge. From web development to VR, HackerMatch is built for all kinds of projects, ensuring you’ll find teammates who share your vision and complement your abilities.

What We Do

HackerMatch streamlines the team-building process with an intuitive swipe-based interface. Simply browse profiles of other hackers, check out their project preferences, skills, and experience, and swipe to connect. Whether you’re looking for a specific role or a diverse skill set, our algorithm ensures that you’re matched with the right people for your next big project.

  • Shared Project Interests: Find teammates who are passionate about the same things you are.
  • Skills & Expertise: Get matched with people who can bring something unique to the table.
  • Team Roles: Easily find front-end, back-end, full-stack developers, or other specialized roles to build a balanced team.

Why We Do It

Hackathons are about collaboration, innovation, and learning from others. We know how tough it can be to find the right team, and that’s why we’re here to help. At HackerMatch, we believe that when the right people come together, great things happen. Our goal is to make that connection seamless, so you can focus on what you do best—hacking, creating, and innovating.
